Method: A method, in the context of programming, refers to a function that is associated with an object.

List Comprehension: List comprehension is a concise way to create lists in programming languages.

Tuple Packing: Tuple packing refers to the process of creating a tuple by grouping multiple values together. 

Tuple: A tuple is an ordered and immutable collection of elements in Python. Tuples are similar to lists but cannot be modified once created. 

Sequence Unpacking:Sequence unpacking is the reverse of tuple packing. It involves extracting individual elements from a tuple or any other iterable and assigning them to separate variables.
